    About Us

    Bread and Water Landscape LLC was started in 2019 by Mr. Jose Paniagua. Mr. Paniagua is a graduate of Cal State Northridge (BS degree) and Cal State San Bernardino (MBA). He brings over 20 years of experience working in the public works arena for another company who performed landscaping, painting, and other maintenance contracts. He started Bread and Water Landscape LLC to work on his own public works contracts, as his own business. He has a C-27, C-61/D38, and C-33 license with the State.

    Our Services

    Bread and Water Landscape LLC offers a wide variety of landscaping, painting, and power washing services to all public works agencies. Mr. Paniagua is experienced and brings a wealth of expertise for any project that may come his way.

        Landscaping

      • Irrigation/sprinklers

        Painting

      • Prepping surfaces

      • Graffiti removal from textured surfaces
